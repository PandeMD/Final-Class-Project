Loan Application and Approval Process

Step 1: OTP and register

Verify the mobile number that you use with your bank or loan accounts and register with us in seconds.

Step 2: Loan Application

To apply for an MSME loan, you will need to complete and submit a loan application form, either online or in person at one of our branches. The application will ask for basic details and information about your business and your financial situation, including your income, assets, and credit history.

 Step 3: Document Submission

In addition to the loan application, you may be required to submit additional documents to support your application. These may include financial statements, tax returns, business plans, and other supporting materials.

 Step 4: Underwriting and Approval

Once your application and supporting documents have been received, they will be reviewed by our underwriting team. The underwriting team will assess the risk of lending to your business and determine whether to approve your loan. The underwriting team will be Sales Executive, Credit Manager, Accounts Head, Operational Executive

 Step 5: Loan Disbursement

If your loan is approved, the funds will be disbursed to you according to the terms of your loan agreement. This may include a one-time lump sum payment or a series of installments.

 Step 6: Repayment

You will be required to make regular payments on your loan according to the terms of your loan agreement. These payments may include principal and interest, as well as any fees or charges associated with the loan.
